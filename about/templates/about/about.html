About Frau Wolle

Guten Tag, Liebchen!

I'm Frau Wolle, a 86-year-old Oma (grandmother) from the charming town of Rothenburg ob der Tauber in Bavaria, Germany. For 64 years, knitting has been my passion, my meditation, and my way of spreading warmth and love to those around me.

My journey with needles and yarn began when I was just a young Mädchen of 22, learning from my own Großmutter. Since then, I've crafted everything from simple scarves to intricate lace shawls, always preferring the challenge of complex patterns over easy projects.

Despite my love for complicated designs, I'm a firm believer in the power of simple tools. Give me a pair of straight needles, some quality wool, and a quiet corner, and I'm in my element. I've never met a cable stitch I didn't like or a color-work pattern I couldn't master!

When I'm not knitting, you can find me tending to my herb garden, baking Apfelstrudel for my grandchildren, or sipping Kaffee with my knitting circle, where we exchange patterns, stories, and a fair bit of local gossip.

Through this blog, I hope to share not just my patterns and techniques, but also the joy, patience, and sense of accomplishment that knitting has brought to my life. So grab your needles, pour yourself a warm drink, and let's create something beautiful together!

Strick mit Liebe (Knit with love),

Frau Wolle
